# PROJECT-BRIEF.md - GROWNA Website

## Overview

**GROWNA** is a premium B2B technology and growth partner brand website. The site positions GROWNA as a high-trust, modern tech company that helps growing businesses with AI solutions, SaaS products, ERP/Odoo, systems integration, automation, and digital transformation.

---

## Business Goals

1. Present GROWNA as a premium, modern, high-trust technology brand
2. Clearly explain services and value proposition
3. Position for business leads and partnerships
4. Create scalable foundation for future expansion (blog, case studies, careers)

---

## Target Audience

- Growing businesses seeking digital transformation
- Enterprises looking for AI/automation solutions
- Companies needing ERP/Odoo implementation
- Businesses seeking systems integration partners

---

## Pages

| Page | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Home** | Hero, trust indicators, services overview, why GROWNA, process, CTA |
| **About** | Company story, mission, values, team placeholder |
| **Services** | Detailed service offerings (AI, SaaS, ERP, Integration, Automation, DX) |
| **Solutions** | Industry or use-case specific solutions |
| **Contact** | Contact form, reach out CTA |

---

## Homepage Sections

1. **Hero** - Strong value proposition, primary/secondary CTAs
2. **Trust/Value Proposition** - Social proof, key metrics
3. **Services Overview** - Grid of 6 core services
4. **Why GROWNA** - Differentiators, trust indicators
5. **Process** - How we work
6. **Featured Solutions** - Highlight key solutions
7. **CTA** - Consultation call-to-action
8. **Footer** - Links, contact info

---

## Design Principles

- Modern premium B2B SaaS aesthetic
- Clean, sharp, confident, elegant
- Strong visual hierarchy
- Smooth motion (Framer Motion) but not excessive
- Dark/light-ready structure
- High-trust enterprise feel
- Conversion-aware layout

---

## Technical Approach

- **Frontend-only** for this phase (no backend)
- **Static content** where possible for performance
- **Component-driven** architecture for reusability
- **Responsive-first** design
- **Performance-optimized** (next/image, lazy loading)

---
